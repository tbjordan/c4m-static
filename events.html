What we offer

Chosen 4 More Ministry is a nondenominational community ministry with in-depth Bible studies producing, empowering, and equipping Christ servants to go into their families, churches, and communities as chosen followers of Christ.

  • Discerning the Voice of God

    This Bible Study (7 sessions) starts Wed., Jan 24th - March 24th invites us to become bolder women of faith in knowing what you hear is from God. You will become more familiar with God's Holy Spirit, His character, language and tone of voice. This is a DVD led study with study guides, journal along with in-depth Biblical teaching from God's Holy word. Meets on Wednesday's from 9:00-11:30am at 982 Yancey Drive., Loris, SC; informal Meet &Greet from 9-9:30 (visit, get coffee, juice and snacks); Bible study begins at 9:30 with Praise and Worship/ Prayer. Open to all women 18+/all denominations; home setting (no childcare at this time) Cost: $15 includes study guide and journal and can pay at 1st session Chosen 4 More Ministry Leader teaching this study is Ginny Stanley .

  • Learning Seminar: When Women Came Running

    Sat., March 24th from 9-11:30AM
    This 3 hour seminar, led by CHOSEN 4 MORE MINISTRY TEAM, invites all women to see themselves as these faithful women. See the Lord’s resurrection unfold thru the eyes and events of 3 brave women who were witnesses at the cross. Understand why these women “ran” to Jesus’s tomb and then “ran” to spread the Good News that HE WAS ALIVE. Most importantly, discover the secrets of how each of us can “run” to Jesus and be just like them!
